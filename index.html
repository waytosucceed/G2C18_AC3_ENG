Types of Sentences


  1. Statement Sentences: These tell us something. They're like sharing a fact with a friend.
    Example: "Hoppy is a little rabbit."
  2. Question Sentences: These ask for information and end with a question mark (?).
    Example: "Do you want to play with Hoppy?"
  3. Command Sentences: These tell someone to do something. They're like friendly instructions.
    Example: "Hop along with your friends, Hoppy!"
  4. Exclamation Sentences: These show big feelings and end with an exclamation mark (!).
    Example: "Wow! Hoppy can jump so high!"
